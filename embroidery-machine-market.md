<p><p>The embroidery machine market is divided into two main types: single head and multi-head machines. Single head machines are designed for smaller operations and are ideal for custom designs and personal projects, as they handle one design at a time. Multi-head machines, on the other hand, have multiple heads that allow for simultaneous stitching of designs, making them suitable for larger businesses aiming for high-volume production. Both types serve distinct needs within the embroidery industry, balancing flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
