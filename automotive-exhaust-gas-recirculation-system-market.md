<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) System market comprises two primary types: Pneumatic EGR Valves and Electric EGR Valves. Pneumatic EGR Valves operate using vacuum pressure to regulate exhaust flow back into the engine, offering a simpler design and lower cost. In contrast, Electric EGR Valves utilize electric actuators for precise control, enhancing responsiveness and efficiency. Both types contribute to reducing nitrogen oxide emissions and improving engine performance, catering to the growing demand for eco-friendly automotive technologies.</p></p>
