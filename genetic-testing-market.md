<p><p>The genetic testing market comprises several types, primarily focusing on analyzers and consumables. They include systems like sequencers and microarrays, which provide precise results. Consumables, on the other hand, refer to the reagents, kits, and sample collection tools necessary for conducting tests. Together, these components support various applications in clinical diagnostics, research, and personalized medicine, driving advancements in healthcare and genetic research.</p></p>
