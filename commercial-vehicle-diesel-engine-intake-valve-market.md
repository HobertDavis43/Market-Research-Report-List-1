<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Diesel Engine Intake Valve market includes various types of valves, such as mono metallic, bimetallic, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made from a single metal material, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Bimetallic valves combine two metals to enhance durability and thermal resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Hollow valves are designed with a hollow structure to reduce weight while maintaining strength, contributing to improved fuel efficiency and engine performance, essential for modern commercial vehicles.</p></p>
