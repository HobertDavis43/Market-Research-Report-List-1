<p><ul><li>Hollow Foam Roller</li><li>Solid Foam Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foam roller market is segmented into two main types: hollow foam rollers and solid foam rollers. Hollow foam rollers are lightweight and typically provide a softer feel, making them ideal for beginners or those seeking gentle myofascial release. In contrast, solid foam rollers are denser and firmer, offering deeper tissue penetration, which is beneficial for advanced users looking for intense muscle relief. Both types cater to different fitness levels and preferences, enhancing recovery and enhancing athletic performance.</p></p>
