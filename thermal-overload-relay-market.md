<p><strong>The Thermal Overload Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Melting Alloy Thermal Overload Relay</li><li>Bimetallic Strip Thermal Overload Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermal Overload Relay market includes two primary types: Melting Alloy Thermal Overload Relay and Bimetallic Strip Thermal Overload Relay. Melting Alloy relays utilize a conductive alloy that melts under excessive heat, triggering a circuit interruption, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Bimetallic Strip relays operate through a bi-metallic strip that bends due to temperature changes, progressively disconnecting the circuit. Both types protect motors from overheating, ensuring safety and operational efficiency in various electrical applications.</p></p>
