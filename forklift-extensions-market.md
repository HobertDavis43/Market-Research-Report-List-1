<p><p>The forklift extensions market can be segmented into two primary types: roller and box. Roller extensions enhance a forklift's capability to handle long or difficult-to-move items by providing a cylindrical support surface that reduces friction. Box extensions, on the other hand, feature a flat platform with side barriers, designed for transporting bulkier materials securely. Both types improve operational efficiency by expanding the range of loads that forklifts can effectively handle, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
