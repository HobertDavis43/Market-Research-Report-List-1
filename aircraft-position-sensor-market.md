<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Angular Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Position Sensor Market consists of two primary types: Linear and Angular sensors. Linear type sensors measure displacement or position along a straight line, crucial for applications like landing gear or flap position sensing. Angular type sensors, on the other hand, detect rotational movement and are essential for applications such as control surfaces or navigation systems. Both types play vital roles in enhancing aircraft safety and performance by providing accurate position data for various flight operations.</p></p>
