<p><strong>The On-Line Water Quality Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrode Method</li><li>Spectrophotometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The On-Line Water Quality Monitoring System market is primarily categorized into two types: Electrode Method and Spectrophotometry. The Electrode Method involves the use of various sensors that measure specific chemical properties of water, offering real-time data on parameters like pH, conductivity, and dissolved oxygen. In contrast, Spectrophotometry utilizes light absorption techniques to analyze water samples, detecting contaminants by measuring the intensity of light at different wavelengths. Both methods provide essential insights for water quality management in various industries.</p></p>
