Water-based modifiers enhance the ink’s stability and application while being environmentally friendly, making them ideal for non-toxic prints. Conversely, solvent-based modifiers improve the performance of inks in various conditions, providing better adhesion and durability, which is crucial for industrial applications and products exposed to harsh environments.</p></p>
