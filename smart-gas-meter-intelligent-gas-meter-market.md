<p><p>Smart gas meters, or intelligent gas meters, enhance gas consumption monitoring through advanced technologies. The market primarily consists of two types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR systems allow for remote data collection, reducing manual meter readings and enabling better billing accuracy. In contrast, AMI systems offer a comprehensive, two-way communication network that supports real-time data exchange, facilitating improved energy management, demand response capabilities, and enhanced customer engagement in energy usage.</p></p>
