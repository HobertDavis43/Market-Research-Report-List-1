<p><p>The semiconductor strain gauges market is primarily divided into two types: naked gauges and backed gauges. Naked gauges consist of only the sensing element, providing high sensitivity and quick response times but requiring careful handling due to their delicate nature. In contrast, backed gauges feature a protective backing material, enhancing durability and ease of installation while slightly compromising sensitivity. Both types are widely used in various applications, including structural monitoring, aerospace, and automotive industries, to precisely measure strain and deformation.</p></p>
