<p><p>The dimethicone market is divided into three main viscosity types: low, medium, and high viscosity dimethicone. Low viscosity dimethicone is valued for its lightweight feel, often used in skincare and haircare products to provide smoothness without greasiness. Medium viscosity dimethicone offers a balanced texture, suitable for versatility in formulations like lotions and creams. High viscosity dimethicone provides a thicker texture, ideal for products requiring more moisture retention and enhanced lubricating properties, making it popular in hair styling and treatment products.</p></p>
