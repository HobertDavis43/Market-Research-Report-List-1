<p><p>The Ceramic UF Membrane market primarily consists of two types: tubular membranes and flat-sheet membranes. Tubular membranes feature a cylindrical structure, allowing for high flow rates and easy cleaning, making them suitable for applications with high solids content. In contrast, flat-sheet membranes offer a larger surface area and are often used in more compact installations. Both types are renowned for their durability, chemical resistance, and ability to produce high-quality filtered water, catering to various industrial and municipal applications.</p></p>
