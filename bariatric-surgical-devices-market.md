<p><p>The bariatric surgical devices market includes two main types: assisting devices and implantable devices. Assisting devices are typically used to aid in weight loss procedures, such as laparoscopic instruments and staplers that facilitate surgery. Implantable devices, on the other hand, refer to products like gastric bands or intragastric balloons that are placed inside the body to restrict food intake or manage gastric volume. Both types play crucial roles in addressing obesity through surgical interventions, catering to various patient needs and surgical techniques.</p></p>
