<p><p>The Portable Operating Tables market comprises four primary types: Manual, Electric, Hydraulic, and Electro-hydraulic. Manual tables rely on manual adjustments for positioning, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Electric tables use powered mechanisms for easy height and tilt adjustments, enhancing convenience and precision. Hydraulic tables incorporate a hydraulic system, allowing smooth and controlled movement, often with a foot-operated feature. Electro-hydraulic tables combine electric and hydraulic functions, providing versatile adjustments at the touch of a button, catering to various surgical requirements and preferences.</p></p>
