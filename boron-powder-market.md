<p><p>The boron powder market is primarily divided into two types: amorphous and crystalline. Amorphous boron powder is characterized by its irregular molecular structure, making it useful in applications requiring enhanced reactivity, such as pyrotechnics and certain chemical reactions. In contrast, crystalline boron powder has a well-defined structure, resulting in greater hardness and stability, making it suitable for applications in ceramics, abrasives, and electronics. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, impacting their demand in various sectors.</p></p>
