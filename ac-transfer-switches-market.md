<p><p>The AC transfer switch market is divided into two primary types: automatic and manual switches. Automatic transfer switches (ATS) enable seamless power transition between utilities and generators, ensuring minimal downtime during outages. They're commonly used in critical applications where reliability is essential. Manual transfer switches, on the other hand, require user intervention to switch power sources, offering a cost-effective solution for less critical operations. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing power management and maintaining system integrity during outages.</p></p>
