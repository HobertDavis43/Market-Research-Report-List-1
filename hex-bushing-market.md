<p><p>The Hex Bushing market features two primary types: Fully Coupled and Screw Thread. Fully Coupled bushings consist of hexagonal-shaped ends that allow for secure connections without additional fasteners, ensuring a tight fit in plumbing and piping systems. In contrast, Screw Thread Type bushings incorporate external threads for easy installation and removal, enabling versatile applications in various industries. Both types cater to different operational requirements, providing solutions for fluid control and pressure regulation in hydraulic and mechanical systems.</p></p>
