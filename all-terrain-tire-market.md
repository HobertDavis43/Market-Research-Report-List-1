<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The All-Terrain Tire market includes two primary types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias Tires are constructed with crisscrossed ply layers, offering durability and strength, ideal for heavy-duty off-road use but with less flexibility and higher heat buildup. Radial Tires feature perpendicular ply layers, providing better fuel efficiency, smoother rides, and improved traction on varied terrains due to enhanced flexibility and heat dissipation. Both cater to off-road enthusiasts but differ in performance, durability, and application suitability.</p></p>
