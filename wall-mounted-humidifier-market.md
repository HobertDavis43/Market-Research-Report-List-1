<p><strong>The Wall-mounted Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wall-mounted humidifiers are categorized into two main types: vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers generate steam by boiling water, releasing warm, moisture-laden air into the environment, making them ideal for colder climates. In contrast, water spray humidifiers disperse fine water droplets into the air, effectively increasing humidity without raising the temperature, suitable for diverse settings. Both types enhance indoor air quality, promoting comfort and health by mitigating dry air issues in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
