<p><ul><li>Mechanical Injection Molding Machines</li><li>Hydraulic Injection Molding Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection molding machines are categorized primarily into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical injection molding machines utilize a servo-driven mechanism for greater precision and energy efficiency, making them suitable for high-speed production and tight tolerances. Hydraulic injection molding machines, on the other hand, rely on hydraulic systems for operation, offering high clamping force and robustness, which is ideal for larger and more complex molds. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, shaping a wide range of plastic products efficiently.</p></p>
