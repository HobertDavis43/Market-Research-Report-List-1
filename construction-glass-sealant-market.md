<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction glass sealant market can be categorized into two main types: single component and multi-component sealants. Single component sealants are ready-to-use products that require no mixing and cure through exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants consist of two or more parts that must be mixed before application, offering enhanced performance and versatility for demanding environments. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring durable, weather-resistant glass installations.</p></p>
