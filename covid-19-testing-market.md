<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 testing market features several key types. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results, enabling immediate decision-making, typically through lateral flow assays. The Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) method offers a more sensitive and quantitative approach, detecting antibodies or antigens, though results take longer. Neutralization Assays assess the effectiveness of antibodies in neutralizing the virus, crucial for vaccine evaluation and therapeutic efficacy. Each testing method serves unique purposes, catering to various clinical and research needs.</p></p>
