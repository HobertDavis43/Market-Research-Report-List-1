<p><p>Scissor lifters are categorized into three main types: hydraulic, mechanical, and pneumatic. Hydraulic scissor lifters utilize fluid pressure to lift heavy loads, offering smooth operation and high lifting capacity; they are commonly used in warehouses. Mechanical scissor lifters rely on a system of levers and gears, providing simplicity and reliability for lighter applications. Pneumatic scissor lifters use compressed air for lifting, allowing for quick adjustments and movements, ideal for environments where speed and efficiency are essential.</p></p>
