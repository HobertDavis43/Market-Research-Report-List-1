<p><p>Environmental testing services encompass various market types aimed at assessing pollutants and contaminants. Organic compounds testing evaluates volatile and semi-volatile substances that can harm air, water, or soil quality. Microbiological contaminants testing identifies harmful microorganisms, ensuring safety in food and water supplies. Residue testing measures remnants of chemicals or pollutants after processes. Heavy metal testing identifies toxic metals like lead and mercury, crucial for environmental safety. Finally, solids testing examines particulate matter in various environments, essential for waste management and pollution control.</p></p>
