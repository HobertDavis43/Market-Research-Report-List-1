<p><p>The ocean current energy electric generator market primarily consists of two types: horizontal shaft generators and vertical shaft generators. Horizontal shaft generators typically utilize a parallel alignment with ocean currents, optimizing efficiency in larger installations. Conversely, vertical shaft generators are designed to capture energy from currents vertically, often being more compact and suitable for smaller-scale applications. Both types harness the kinetic energy of ocean currents to produce electricity, contributing to renewable energy solutions and sustainable power generation.</p></p>
