<p><strong>The MRI Coils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phased-array</li><li>Quadrature</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MRI coils are specialized devices used to receive signals from the body during magnetic resonance imaging. The phased-array coil features multiple receiver elements arranged in a grid, enhancing image quality by improving signal reception and spatial resolution. Quadrature coils, on the other hand, utilize a combination of signals to capture more data simultaneously, leading to faster imaging and improved signal-to-noise ratios. Both types are essential for optimizing MRI performance, catering to various clinical needs and applications.</p></p>
