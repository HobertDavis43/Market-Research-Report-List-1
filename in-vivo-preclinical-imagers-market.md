<p><p>The in vivo preclinical imagers market encompasses various imaging modalities used for research in animal models. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides high-resolution anatomical images and functional insights. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) allows for metabolic and biochemical tracking of processes. Micro Computed Tomography (Micro-CT) offers detailed 3D imaging for structural analysis. Optical Imaging utilizes fluorescent or bioluminescent markers for real-time visualization of biological processes. Other modalities may include ultrasound and radar imaging, each catering to specific research needs and applications.</p></p>
