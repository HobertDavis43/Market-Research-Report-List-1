<p><p>The powder waterproof coating market primarily consists of two main types: liquid waterproof coatings and dry waterproof coatings. Liquid waterproof coatings are versatile, easy to apply, and suitable for various surfaces, providing a seamless and flexible barrier against moisture. In contrast, dry waterproof coatings, often used in powder form, are applied through techniques like spraying or electrostatic deposition, offering durable, long-lasting protection. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing the longevity and integrity of structures exposed to water.</p></p>
