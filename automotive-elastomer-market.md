<p><p>The automotive elastomer market encompasses various types of rubber materials essential for vehicle components. Natural rubber offers excellent resilience and flexibility, while Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (SBR) is favored for its strength and wear resistance. Nitrile elastomers provide oil resistance, making them ideal for fuel systems. Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) is used in weather seals due to its durability against environmental factors. Silicone rubber is valued for high-temperature applications, and fluoroelastomers are known for chemical resistance. Styrene Block Copolymers combine elasticity and toughness, while other specialized elastomers serve niche applications in the industry.</p></p>
