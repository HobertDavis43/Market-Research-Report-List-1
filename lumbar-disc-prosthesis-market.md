<p><strong>The Lumbar Disc Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-on-metal</li><li>Metal-on-biopolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lumbar disc prosthesis market is primarily categorized into two types: metal-on-metal and metal-on-biopolymer devices. Metal-on-metal prostheses utilize durable metallic components to mimic natural disc function, providing excellent strength and stability. In contrast, metal-on-biopolymer discs combine metal frameworks with biopolymer materials, offering a balance of flexibility and biocompatibility, which enhances patient comfort. Each type addresses distinct patient needs and preferences, influencing surgical outcomes and longevity of the implants in spinal procedures.</p></p>
