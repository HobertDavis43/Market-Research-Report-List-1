<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Leaf Type</li><li>Swing Pistol Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile Electric Vacuum Pump (EVP) market consists of three main types: diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing piston type. Diaphragm-type pumps use a flexible membrane to create vacuum pressure, offering compactness and efficiency. Leaf-type pumps utilize a series of flexible blades to generate suction, known for their lightweight design. Swing piston-type pumps employ a reciprocating piston mechanism to produce vacuum, providing higher capacity and reliability. Each type caters to different vehicle needs, optimizing performance and efficiency in modern automobiles.</p></p>
