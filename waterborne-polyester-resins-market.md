<p><ul><li>Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li><li>Not Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Waterborne Polyester Resins market is divided into two primary types: saturated and unsaturated waterborne polyester resins. Saturated waterborne polyester resins are characterized by their fully hydrogenated structures, offering enhanced chemical resistance, durability, and stability, making them suitable for a range of applications such as coatings and adhesives. In contrast, unsaturated waterborne polyester resins contain double bonds, enabling them to cure and cross-link, resulting in stronger and more versatile products for applications in automotive and construction industries.</p></p>
