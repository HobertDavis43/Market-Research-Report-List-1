<p><p>The animal anti-infectives market is categorized into two primary types: injection and unguent. Injection formulations are typically used for systemic treatment, delivering medications directly into the bloodstream for rapid action against infections in livestock and pets. Unguents, on the other hand, are topical applications designed for localized treatment, often used to manage skin infections or wounds. Both forms are essential in veterinary medicine to ensure the health and productivity of animals while preventing the spread of infectious diseases.</p></p>
