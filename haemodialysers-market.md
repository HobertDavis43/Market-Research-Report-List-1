<p><strong>The Haemodialysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The haemodialysers market is primarily categorized into low flux and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes allow smaller molecules and waste products to pass through while retaining larger proteins and cells, making them suitable for patients with stable conditions. In contrast, high flux membranes facilitate the removal of larger solutes and middle molecules, offering enhanced efficiency for patients with more complex needs, such as those with high toxin levels. Both types play crucial roles in managing renal failure through dialysis therapy.</p></p>
