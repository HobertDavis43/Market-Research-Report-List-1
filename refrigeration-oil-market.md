<p><strong>The Refrigeration Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mineral Oil</li><li>Synthetic Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigeration oil market is primarily divided into two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, offers cost-effectiveness and is compatible with specific refrigerants. However, it has lower stability and performance at extreme temperatures. On the other hand, synthetic oil is chemically engineered, providing superior thermal stability, lower flammability, and enhanced lubrication properties, making it suitable for high-performance systems. Both types cater to varying demands in refrigeration applications, impacting efficiency and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
