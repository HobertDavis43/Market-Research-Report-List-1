<p><ul><li>Rosin Based Pastes</li><li>Water Soluble Fluxes</li><li>No-Clean Flux</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soldering flux paste is essential in electronics for improving solder adhesion and preventing oxidation. Rosin-based pastes, derived from natural resins, offer excellent soldering properties but may require cleaning afterward. Water-soluble fluxes, designed for easy removal, are effective in preventing residue issues during assembly but need thorough cleaning. No-clean fluxes leave minimal residues and are suitable for applications where cleaning is impractical, making them popular in high-volume production scenarios. Each type caters to specific requirements in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
