<p><p>The Tung Oil market is primarily divided into two types: Raw Tung Oil and Boiled Tung Oil. Boiled Tung Oil, on the other hand, is treated with heat and additives to enhance its drying time and improve its durability, offering a richer finish for furniture and other applications.</p></p>
