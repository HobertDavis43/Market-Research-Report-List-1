<p><p>The Transcranial Magnetic Stimulator (TMS) market consists of several types, each serving distinct therapeutic purposes. sTMS (single pulse TMS) delivers a single magnetic pulse for research and diagnostic applications. pTMS (paired pulse TMS) assesses cortical excitability by delivering two pulses in quick succession. rTMS (repetitive TMS) involves multiple pulses to treat depression and other neurological disorders. nTMS (navigated TMS) utilizes neuronavigation technology for precise targeting, enhancing treatment efficacy in various conditions such as stroke rehabilitation and pain management.</p></p>
