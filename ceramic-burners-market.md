<p><ul><li>Grid Ceramic Burner</li><li>Sleeve Ceramic Burner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Burners market features two primary types: Grid Ceramic Burners and Sleeve Ceramic Burners. Grid Ceramic Burners consist of a grid-like structure that evenly distributes heat, making them ideal for high-efficiency applications. In contrast, Sleeve Ceramic Burners are tubular in design, offering excellent thermal stability and resistance to thermal shock, making them suitable for various industrial uses. Both types are increasingly favored for their durability, energy efficiency, and performance in diverse heating applications.</p></p>
