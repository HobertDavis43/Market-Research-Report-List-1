<p><strong>The Coast Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Band Radar</li><li>S-Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Coast Radar market is primarily divided into two types: X-Band Radar and S-Band Radar. X-Band Radar operates at higher frequencies, offering superior resolution and detailed imagery, making it ideal for short-range surveillance and small target detection, such as in harbors and coastal areas. In contrast, S-Band Radar operates at lower frequencies, providing longer range coverage and better penetration through weather conditions, which is beneficial for tracking larger vessels and monitoring maritime traffic over extended distances. Both types play crucial roles in maritime safety and navigation.</p></p>
