<p><strong>The 3D Scanning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D scanning system market comprises two main types: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type scanners utilize probes or mechanical arms to physically touch and measure the object, ensuring high precision but often requiring surface preparation. In contrast, non-contact type scanners employ technologies like laser, structured light, or photogrammetry to capture surface details without direct contact, allowing for faster scanning of delicate or complex shapes. Both technologies serve various industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and cultural heritage preservation.</p></p>
