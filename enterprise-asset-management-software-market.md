<p><p>Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) software is typically categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EAM software is hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, reduced IT overhead, and easier access from various locations. Conversely, on-premise EAM solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and security, but requiring significant investment in infrastructure and maintenance.
