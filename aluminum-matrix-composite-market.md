<p><p>The Aluminum Matrix Composite (AMC) market is categorized into three types: continuous, discontinuous, and particle-reinforced composites. Continuous composites involve long strands or fibers uniformly distributed for enhanced strength and stability, typically used in aerospace and automotive applications. Discontinuous composites incorporate shorter fibers or segments, offering versatility and easier processing for various engineering applications. Particle-reinforced composites utilize fine particles for lightweight structures and improved wear resistance, commonly used in consumer goods and high-performance components, balancing cost and performance.</p></p>
