<p><ul><li>Normally Open (H)</li><li>Normally Closed (D)</li><li>Transformation (Z)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal reed relay market can be categorized into three types: Normally Open (H), Normally Closed (D), and Transformation (Z). Normally Open (H) relays require a signal to activate, closing the circuit when energized. Conversely, Normally Closed (D) relays maintain a closed circuit and open upon receiving a signal, providing a default state of conductivity. Transformation (Z) relays are designed for specific applications, allowing for versatile switching configurations. Each type serves distinct functions in various electronic and automation applications.</p></p>
