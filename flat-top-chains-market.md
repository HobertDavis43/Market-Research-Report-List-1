<p><strong>The Flat Top Chains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Running Flat Top Chains</li><li>Side Flexing Flat Top Chains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat top chains are essential in various industrial applications, categorized into two main types: straight running and side flexing chains. Straight running flat top chains offer smooth, linear movement ideal for conveyor systems that require straight paths. In contrast, side flexing flat top chains are designed with articulating links, allowing them to navigate corners and curves, making them suitable for more complex layouts. Both types are crucial for efficient material handling, enhancing productivity across multiple industries, including food processing and packaging.</p></p>
