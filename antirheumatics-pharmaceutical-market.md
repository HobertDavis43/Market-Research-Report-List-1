<p><p>The antirheumatics pharmaceutical market comprises various types of medications designed to alleviate symptoms of rheumatic diseases. Slow Acting Drugs, such as DMARDs, provide long-term control of rheumatoid arthritis. Immunological Preparations, including biological agents, modulate or enhance the immune response, targeting underlying causes of rheumatic conditions. Together, these categories optimize patient management and improve quality of life for sufferers.</p></p>
