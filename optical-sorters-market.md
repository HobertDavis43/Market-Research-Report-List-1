<p><p>The optical sorters market is primarily segmented into two types: Chute-Type Color Sorter Machines and Belt-Type Color Sorter Machines. Chute-Type Sorters utilize a downward flow mechanism to separate materials based on color, ideal for bulk processing. In contrast, Belt-Type Sorters use a conveyor belt system that allows for continuous sorting, suitable for a wide range of materials and providing enhanced flexibility. Both types enhance operational efficiency and product quality in industries such as food processing and recycling.</p></p>
