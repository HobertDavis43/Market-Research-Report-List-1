<p><strong>The IoT Smart Ultrasonic Proximity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam</li><li>Retro-Reflective</li><li>Diffuse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT smart ultrasonic proximity sensors market includes three main types: Through-Beam, Retro-Reflective, and Diffuse. Through-Beam sensors emit a continuous sound wave from a transmitter to a receiver, detecting objects based on interruption of the beam. Retro-Reflective sensors send waves that bounce back from a reflector, allowing detection of objects without a direct line. Diffuse sensors work by detecting echoes from nearby objects, suitable for various applications where precise distance measurement and obstacle detection are required.</p></p>
