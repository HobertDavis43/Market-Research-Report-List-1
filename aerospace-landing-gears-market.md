<p><p>The aerospace landing gear market comprises two main types: main landing gear and nose/tail landing gear. Main landing gear is situated under the aircraft's fuselage, providing the primary support during takeoff, landing, and ground operations. Nose landing gear, located at the front, aids in steering and stabilizing the aircraft during taxiing, while tail landing gear is less common, primarily seen in some military and specialized aircraft. Each type serves essential functions, ensuring safety and performance in aerospace operations.</p></p>
