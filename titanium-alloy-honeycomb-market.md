<p><p>The titanium alloy honeycomb market is categorized into monolayer and multilayer types. Monolayer titanium alloy honeycomb consists of a single layer, offering lightweight yet strong structural support, ideal for applications requiring simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, multilayer titanium alloy honeycomb features multiple layers, enhancing strength and insulation properties, making it suitable for more demanding environments where durability and thermal resistance are crucial. Both types are utilized in aerospace, automotive, and marine industries for their superior performance and weight-saving characteristics.</p></p>
