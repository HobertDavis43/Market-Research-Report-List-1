<p><p>The Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) market encompasses three main types: monochrome, multi-color, and full-color displays. Monochrome OLEDs feature a single color output, typically used in simpler devices like watches and labels, offering high contrast and low power consumption. Multi-color OLEDs enable the display of multiple colors but are limited in palette, making them suitable for applications like indicators. Full-color OLEDs provide a wide spectrum of colors, ideal for televisions, smartphones, and digital signage, delivering vibrant images and superior picture quality.</p></p>
