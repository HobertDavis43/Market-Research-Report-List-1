<p><strong>The CO2 Gas Sensor Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) CO2 Sensors</li><li>EC (Electrochemical) CO2 Sensors</li><li>PAS (Photoacoustic Spectroscopy) CO2 Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 gas sensor module market includes various types of sensors with distinct technologies. NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) sensors use infrared light absorption to detect CO2 levels, offering high accuracy and stability. Electrochemical (EC) sensors utilize chemical reactions to measure gas concentrations, often being compact and cost-effective. Photoacoustic Spectroscopy (PAS) sensors detect gas through sound waves produced by absorbed light, providing sensitive measurements. Other sensor types may include MEMS and optical sensors, catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
