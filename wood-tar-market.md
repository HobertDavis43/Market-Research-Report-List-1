<p><p>The wood tar market is categorized into three primary types: resinous tars, hardwood tars, and others. Resinous tars are derived from coniferous trees and are known for their high resin content, making them suitable for applications like varnishes and preservatives. Hardwood tars originate from deciduous trees and often possess unique chemical properties, making them valuable for industrial uses. The "others" category encompasses various specialized tars sourced from different wood types or methods, catering to niche markets and specific applications.</p></p>
