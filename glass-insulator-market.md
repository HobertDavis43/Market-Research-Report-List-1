<p><ul><li>Suspension Glass Insulators</li><li>Pin Glass Insulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass insulator market is segmented into two primary types: suspension glass insulators and pin glass insulators. Suspension glass insulators are designed to support conductors in overhead power lines, providing stability and durability against environmental stress. They are typically used in high-voltage applications. Pin glass insulators, on the other hand, are mounted on poles and serve to hold wires in place, ideal for lower voltage lines. Both types ensure efficient electrical insulation and reliability in power transmission systems.</p></p>
