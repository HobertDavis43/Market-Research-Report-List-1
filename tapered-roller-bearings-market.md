<p><p>Tapered roller bearings are categorized into various types based on configuration. Double-row tapered roller bearings offer higher load-bearing capacity and stability, making them suitable for heavy machinery. Four-row tapered roller bearings provide exceptional load distribution and are often utilized in industrial applications.
