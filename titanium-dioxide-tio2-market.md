<p><strong>The Titanium Dioxide (TiO2) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium Dioxide (TiO2) is primarily produced through two processes: the sulfate and chloride processes. The sulfate process involves the reaction of titanium ores with sulfuric acid, yielding high-volume production suitable for various applications. The chloride process, on the other hand, utilizes titanium tetrachloride, derived from titanium ores, to produce a higher purity TiO2. Each method influences the TiO2 market differently, with the chloride process typically commanding higher prices due to its superior quality and lower environmental impact compared to the sulfate route.</p></p>
