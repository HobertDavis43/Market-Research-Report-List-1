<p><p>Fixed asset software is categorized into three primary market types: On-Premise, Web-Based, and Cloud-Based. On-Premise solutions are installed locally on a company's hardware, offering high control but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. Web-Based software operates through a web browser, providing flexibility and ease of access without heavy installation demands. Cloud-Based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access assets from anywhere with internet connectivity, often featuring automatic updates and scalability advantages for businesses.</p></p>
