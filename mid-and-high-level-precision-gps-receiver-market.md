<p><ul><li>Single Frequency</li><li>Dual Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mid and high-level precision GPS receivers include two primary market types: single frequency and dual frequency. Single frequency receivers operate on one frequency signal, typically offering basic positioning accuracy suitable for general applications. In contrast, dual frequency receivers utilize two different frequency signals, allowing for improved accuracy and reduced errors caused by atmospheric conditions. This makes dual frequency receivers ideal for applications requiring higher precision, such as surveying, aviation, and autonomous navigation, providing more reliable and accurate positioning solutions.</p></p>
