<p><p>The auto orbital polishers market is primarily divided into pneumatic and electric types. Pneumatic polishers use compressed air, offering lightweight and continuous operation, ideal for professionals needing sustained performance. They excel in providing consistent power but require an air compressor. Electric polishers, on the other hand, operate on electricity, catering to a broader audience due to their ease of use and portability. They are versatile for both professionals and DIY enthusiasts, making them widely popular in the automotive detailing sector.</p></p>
