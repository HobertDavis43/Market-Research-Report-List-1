<p><ul><li>Leaflet Clamping</li><li>Annulus Retraction</li><li>Increase The Matching Area</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tricuspid Valve Interventional Therapy Market encompasses several therapeutic approaches to improve valve function. Leaflet clamping involves reducing valve regurgitation by mechanically approximating the valve leaflets. Annulus retraction refers to the technique of narrowing the valve annulus to enhance coaptation and reduce leakage. Increasing the matching area focuses on augmenting the contact zone between valve leaflets to improve sealing and performance. These strategies aim to address tricuspid valve incompetence and enhance patient outcomes through minimally invasive interventions.</p></p>
