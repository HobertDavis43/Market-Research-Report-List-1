<p><p>Leaf-spine fabric switches are designed for data center architectures that enhance network performance and scalability. The leaf switch connects to devices like servers and storage, aggregating traffic before forwarding it to the spine switch. The spine switch acts as a backbone, interconnecting multiple leaf switches, ensuring efficient data flow. This architecture minimizes latency and provides high bandwidth, accommodating growing data demands. The leaf-spine setup is favored in cloud computing and large-scale enterprise environments for its flexibility and resilience.</p></p>
