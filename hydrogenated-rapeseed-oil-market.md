<p><p>Hydrogenated rapeseed oil is produced by the process of hydrogenation, which involves the addition of hydrogen to liquid rapeseed oil, transforming it into a more stable solid or semi-solid fat. This modification enhances its shelf life, improves texture, and increases resistance to oxidation, making it popular in various food applications such as margarine, baking, and frying. The growing demand for healthy and trans-fat-free alternatives in the food industry has significantly driven its market.</p><p>The Hydrogenated Rapeseed Oil Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period. Furthermore, the increasing prevalence of plant-based diets is fueling interest in vegetable oils, including hydrogenated rapeseed oil, as a substitute for animal fats.
