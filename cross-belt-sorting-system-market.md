<p><ul><li>Horizontal Cross Belt Sorter</li><li>Vertical Cross Belt Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cross Belt Sorting System market is categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical cross belt sorters. Horizontal cross belt sorters transport items on a flat surface, making them ideal for high-speed, high-volume sorting in warehouses and distribution centers. In contrast, vertical cross belt sorters utilize an inclined design to efficiently manage space and facilitate gravity-driven sorting, suitable for handling various product sizes. Both types enhance operational efficiency and accuracy in sorting processes across industries.</p></p>
