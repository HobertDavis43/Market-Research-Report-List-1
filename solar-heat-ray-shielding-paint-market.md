<p><p>The Solar Heat-ray Shielding Paint Market is categorized into two main types: Heat Ray Reflection and Heat Ray Absorption. Heat Ray Reflection paints are designed to deflect solar radiation, minimizing heat transfer to surfaces, thereby enhancing energy efficiency and comfort in buildings. In contrast, Heat Ray Absorption paints are formulated to absorb solar heat, converting it into thermal energy for various applications, such as heating water. Both types aim to reduce energy consumption and improve indoor climate control.</p></p>
