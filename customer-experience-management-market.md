<p><p>Customer Experience Management (CEM) encompasses two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based CEM allows businesses to utilize software hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, flexibility, and lower upfront costs. This model enables real-time updates and accessibility from anywhere. In contrast, on-premise CEM solutions involve installing software locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and security but requiring higher initial investments and maintenance. Each type serves different organizational needs based on resources and strategic goals.</p></p>
