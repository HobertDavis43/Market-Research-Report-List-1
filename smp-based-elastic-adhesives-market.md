<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMP-based elastic adhesives market includes two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed formulations that cure upon exposure to moisture or heat, offering ease of use and quick application. In contrast, two-component adhesives require a separate hardener to initiate the curing process, allowing for customizable properties and improved performance for specialized applications. Both types provide excellent bonding strength and flexibility, making them suitable for diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
