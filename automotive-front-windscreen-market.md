<p><strong>The Automotive Front Windscreen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive front windscreen market primarily features two types of glass: laminated and tempered. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers, providing enhanced safety and sound insulation, making it resistant to shattering. This type is commonly used in windshields. Tempered glass, on the other hand, undergoes heat treatment to increase durability and strength, shattering into small, less harmful pieces upon impact. While laminated glass is favored for its safety features, tempered glass is valued for its aesthetic appeal and lower manufacturing costs.</p></p>
