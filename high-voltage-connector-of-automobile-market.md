<p><p>The high voltage connector market in automobiles includes three main types: line-to-line, line-to-board, and board-to-board connectors. Line-to-line connectors facilitate connections between two wires or cables, ensuring efficient power transfer. Line-to-board connectors connect wiring harnesses directly to circuit boards, enabling effective communication and power distribution. Board-to-board connectors link multiple circuit boards within a vehicle, promoting modular design and flexibility. Each type plays a critical role in the reliability and efficiency of electric and hybrid vehicle systems.</p></p>
