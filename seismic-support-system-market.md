<p><strong>The Seismic Support System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cylindrical Support</li><li>Bushing Support</li><li>Conical Support</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Seismic support systems are essential for safeguarding structures against seismic forces. Cylindrical supports provide uniform strength and flexibility, accommodating lateral movements. Bushing supports enhance isolation by absorbing vibrations, often utilizing rubber or elastomeric materials to dampen shock. Conical supports, designed with a tapered shape, offer stability while allowing for angular displacement, making them suitable for dynamic environments. Each type serves a unique function in mitigating seismic impact, ensuring the integrity and safety of buildings during earthquakes.</p></p>
