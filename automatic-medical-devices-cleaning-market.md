<p><p>The automatic medical devices cleaning market encompasses two primary types of detergents: enzymatic and non-enzymatic. Enzymatic detergents contain specific enzymes that effectively break down organic matter, such as blood and proteins, ensuring thorough cleaning of medical instruments. In contrast, non-enzymatic detergents rely on chemical formulations to clean without the enzymatic action, making them suitable for general cleaning purposes. Both types are essential for maintaining hygiene and preventing infection in healthcare settings, contributing significantly to patient safety.</p></p>
