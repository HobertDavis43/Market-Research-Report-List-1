<p><p>The double block and bleed valves market encompasses various types, primarily ball valves and needle valves. Ball valves feature a spherical disc that controls flow, ensuring quick shut-off and reliability in high-pressure applications. Needle valves, on the other hand, provide precise flow regulation due to their tapered design, making them ideal for fine adjustments in low-flow scenarios. Both types are essential in managing fluids in pipelines, enhancing safety by allowing for effective isolation and draining processes.</p></p>
