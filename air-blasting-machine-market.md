<p><p>The air blasting machine market features three primary types: Gravity Suction Type, Direct Pressure Type, and Blower Type. Gravity Suction Type utilizes gravitational force to draw abrasive media, making it ideal for delicate applications. Direct Pressure Type delivers a constant and powerful flow of abrasive via compressed air, suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Blower Type uses a high-capacity air blower to propel abrasive materials, offering efficient operation for larger-scale projects. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and application requirements.</p></p>
