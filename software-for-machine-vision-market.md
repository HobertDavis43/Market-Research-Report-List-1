<p><p>The software for machine vision market is categorized into 2D and 3D vision systems. 2D vision systems use cameras to capture flat images, analyzing them for quality control, identification, and measurement tasks. These systems are cost-effective and widely used in manufacturing. In contrast, 3D vision systems utilize advanced technologies, such as laser triangulation or structured light, to create depth maps, allowing for more complex inspections, robot guidance, and object recognition. Together, they enhance automation and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
