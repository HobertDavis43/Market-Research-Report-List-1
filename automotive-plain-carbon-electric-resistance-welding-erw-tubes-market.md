<p><ul><li>Straight Seam Welded Pipe</li><li>Spiral Welded Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Plain Carbon Electric Resistance Welding (ERW) Tubes market includes Straight Seam Welded Pipe and Spiral Welded Pipe. Straight Seam Welded Pipe is manufactured by rolling steel into a cylindrical shape and welding the seam longitudinally, offering uniformity and strength, ideal for automotive structural applications. Spiral Welded Pipe is created by helically winding steel strips and welding the seam, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness, suitable for larger diameter automotive components. Both types are widely used in automotive manufacturing for their durability and precision.</p></p>
