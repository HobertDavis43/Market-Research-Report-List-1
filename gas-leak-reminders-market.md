<p><p>Gas leak reminders encompass various market types based on sensing technologies. Electrochemical sensors detect specific gas concentrations through chemical reactions, providing accuracy for hazardous gases. Semiconductor sensors utilize changes in electrical conductivity to identify gas presence, often in low-cost applications. Ultrasonic sensors detect leaks by measuring sound waves produced by escaping gases. Infrared sensors analyze gas absorption patterns, effectively identifying gases without contact. Lastly, the "Others" category includes alternative technologies like photoionization and catalytic sensors, expanding the market's diversity.</p></p>
