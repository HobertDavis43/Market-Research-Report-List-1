<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The 4D Printing Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet Printing</li><li>Melt Deposition Modeling (FDM)</li><li>Direct Ink Writing (DIW)</li><li>Stereolithography (SLA)</li><li>Electron Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 4D printing technology market encompasses various types of printing techniques which enable the creation of materials that can change shape or function over time. Inkjet printing utilizes droplets of material to form intricate designs. Melt Deposition Modeling (FDM) extrudes thermoplastic filaments, enabling layer-by-layer construction. Direct Ink Writing (DIW) deposits viscous inks for customizable shapes. Stereolithography (SLA) uses a laser to cure liquid resin, creating detailed objects. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) and Selective Laser Melting (SLM) employ focused energy sources to fuse metal powders, ideal for complex geometries.</p></p>
