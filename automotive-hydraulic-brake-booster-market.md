<p><p>The automotive hydraulic brake booster market encompasses various components essential for enhancing braking performance. The booster body houses the entire assembly, while the booster piston amplifies the driver's input force. The piston return spring ensures the piston resets after braking, contributing to system reliability. The reaction mechanism provides feedback to the driver, enhancing control, and the control valve mechanism regulates fluid flow within the system. Together, these elements work synergistically to improve braking efficiency and safety in vehicles.</p></p>
