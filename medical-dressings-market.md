<p><p>The medical dressings market encompasses various types designed for wound care. Natural gauze provides breathable support and is often used for light to moderate wounds. Synthetic fiber dressings offer enhanced absorption and durability. Polymembrane dressings create a moisture-retentive barrier, while foaming polycluster dressings provide cushioning and protection for deeper wounds. Water colloid dressings promote a moist healing environment. Alginate dressings, derived from seaweed, are highly absorbent for exudating wounds. Other market types include hydrogel, silicone, and antimicrobial dressings.</p></p>
