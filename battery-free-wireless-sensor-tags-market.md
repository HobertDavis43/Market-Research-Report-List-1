<p><p>The battery-free wireless sensor tags market consists of three main types: active tags, passive tags, and semi-active tags. Active tags generate their own power, enabling longer range and more complex functionalities. Passive tags, on the other hand, harvest energy from external sources, making them lightweight and simple with limited range. Semi-active tags combine both methods, drawing power from an external source while utilizing onboard batteries for enhanced performance. Each type serves different applications across industries like logistics, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
