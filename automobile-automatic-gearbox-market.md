<p><ul><li>Electric Power Steering Gearbox</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile automatic gearbox market features two primary types: Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS) gearboxes. EPS utilizes an electric motor to assist steering, offering improved fuel efficiency and ease of use with reduced weight. Conversely, HPS relies on hydraulic fluid pressure generated by a pump, providing strong steering assistance but potentially impacting fuel efficiency due to its reliance on the engine. Both systems aim to enhance driving comfort and vehicle control, catering to diverse consumer preferences and automotive designs.</p></p>
