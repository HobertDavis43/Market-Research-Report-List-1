<p><strong>The Acrylic Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Spinning Acrylic Fibers</li><li>Dry Spinning Acrylic Fibers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic fibers market is primarily segmented into wet spinning and dry spinning methods. Wet spinning involves dissolving acrylic polymer in a solvent to form a solution, which is then extruded into a coagulation bath, producing fibers with excellent dyeability and strength. In contrast, dry spinning uses a solvent to create a polymer solution that is evaporated once extruded, yielding lightweight and soft fibers. Each type serves various applications, including textiles, home furnishings, and industrial materials, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
