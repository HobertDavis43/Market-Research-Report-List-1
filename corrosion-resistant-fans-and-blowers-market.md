<p><p>The corrosion-resistant fans and blowers market is segmented into three main types: axial fans and blowers, centrifugal fans and blowers, and others. </p><p>Axial fans and blowers move air parallel to the fan axis, offering high efficiency for large volumes of air, making them ideal for applications in harsh environments. Centrifugal fans and blowers, on the other hand, use a rotating impeller to increase air pressure and are suitable for high resistance to corrosive elements. The "others" category encompasses specialized designs tailored for unique industrial needs.</p></p>
