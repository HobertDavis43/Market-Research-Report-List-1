<p><p>A steel electrode is a metal rod or wire used in welding processes to create an electric arc that melts and fuses metals together. Steel electrodes are widely used in industries such as construction, automotive, shipbuilding, and manufacturing due to their durability, strength, and versatility.</p><p>The Steel Electrode Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand from infrastructure development, industrial automation, and the expansion of the manufacturing sector.
