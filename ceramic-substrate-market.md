<p><p>The ceramic substrate market comprises various types, including alumina (Al2O3), aluminum nitride (AlN), and silicon nitride substrates. Alumina substrates are widely used due to their excellent electrical insulation and thermal conductivity, making them suitable for electronic applications. AlN substrates offer superior thermal conductivity and are ideal for high-power electronics. Silicon nitride substrates, known for their mechanical strength and thermal stability, are utilized in advanced electronic devices and applications requiring durable performance. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
