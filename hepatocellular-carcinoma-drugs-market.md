<p><p>The hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) drugs market comprises several treatment modalities, including brachytherapy, chemotherapy, and local ablation therapy. Brachytherapy involves delivering localized radiation directly to the tumor, minimizing damage to surrounding tissue. Chemotherapy utilizes systemic medications to target cancer cells throughout the body, though it may have limited efficacy in HCC. Local ablation therapy encompasses techniques like radiofrequency and microwave ablation to destroy tumors using heat or cold. Each approach offers distinct advantages depending on tumor size, location, and patient health.</p></p>
