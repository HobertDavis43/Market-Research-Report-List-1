<p><p>The dental extraoral X-ray market includes various imaging types, primarily panoramic and cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT). In contrast, CBCT offers advanced three-dimensional imaging, allowing for detailed analysis of oral structures, making it ideal for complex cases like implants and orthodontics. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities and treatment planning in dental practices.</p></p>
