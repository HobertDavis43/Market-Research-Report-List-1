<p><p>The cutaneous fibrosis drugs market includes three main types: corticosteroids, anti-fibrotic drugs, and immunoglobulins. Corticosteroids reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms associated with fibrosis. Anti-fibrotic drugs work by inhibiting the excessive formation of collagen and scar tissue, aiming to restore normal skin texture. Immunoglobulins function by modulating the immune response, potentially reducing fibrosis progression. Together, these therapies target various mechanisms of cutaneous fibrosis, offering patients diverse treatment options to manage their condition effectively.</p></p>
