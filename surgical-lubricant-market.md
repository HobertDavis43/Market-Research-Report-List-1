<p><p>The surgical lubricant market is categorized into three main types: water-based, oil-based, and silicone-based lubricants. Water-based lubricants are easily washable and non-irritating, making them suitable for various medical procedures. Oil-based lubricants provide enhanced viscosity and longer-lasting lubrication, ideal for prolonged interventions. Silicone-based lubricants offer excellent biocompatibility and are resistant to breakdown, ensuring smooth application and reducing friction. Each type caters to specific surgical needs, enhancing procedural efficiency and patient comfort during medical interventions.</p></p>
