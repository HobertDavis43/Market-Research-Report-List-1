<p><p>The Cardiopulmonary Bypass System market is primarily segmented into two types: Conventional Cardiopulmonary Bypass Systems and Mini Cardiopulmonary Bypass Systems. Conventional systems are large, often used for major surgeries, providing comprehensive heart-lung support by oxygenating blood and removing carbon dioxide. In contrast, Mini systems are smaller, designed for less invasive procedures, offering similar functions with reduced complexity and enhanced portability. Both types are crucial for maintaining circulation and oxygenation during cardiac surgeries, each fitting specific surgical requirements.</p></p>
