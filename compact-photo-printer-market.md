<p><strong>The Compact Photo Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>No Ink Printing</li><li>Sublimation Print</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compact photo printer market is primarily divided into two types: no ink printing and sublimation printing. No ink printing technology utilizes ZINK (Zero Ink) or dye-based methods, eliminating the need for traditional ink cartridges. Instead, it uses special paper that activates color during the printing process. Sublimation printing, on the other hand, involves transferring dye onto photo paper through heat, producing vibrant, long-lasting images. Both technologies cater to consumers seeking portable solutions for high-quality photo printing.</p></p>
