<p><p>The Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin (SAC) market comprises two main types: Gel Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin and Macroporous Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin. Gel SAC resins feature a compact structure, ideal for applications requiring high capacity and low operating pressure, effectively removing cations from solutions. In contrast, Macroporous SAC resins possess larger pores, facilitating higher flow rates and enhanced mass transfer, making them suitable for processes involving larger molecules or higher ionic strengths, such as industrial wastewater treatment.</p></p>
