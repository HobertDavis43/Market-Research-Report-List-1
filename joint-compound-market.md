<p><ul><li>Single Joint Compound</li><li>Multi Joint Compound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The joint compound market consists of two primary types: single joint compound and multi joint compound. Single joint compound is designed for filling and finishing joints in drywall installations, offering a quick-drying and easy-to-sand solution suitable for minor repairs. In contrast, multi joint compound is a versatile product formulated for various applications, including taping, finishing, and texturing, providing enhanced durability and flexibility. Both types cater to different user needs in construction and remodeling projects.</p></p>
