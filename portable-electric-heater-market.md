<p><p>The portable electric heater market comprises various types designed for different heating needs. Oil-filled heaters provide long-lasting warmth through heated oil, ideal for maintaining consistent temperatures. Halogen heaters utilize infrared technology for instant heat, suitable for targeted warming in smaller spaces. Other types may include ceramic or fan-based heaters, each catering to specific preferences and room sizes, contributing to a diverse selection for consumers.</p></p>
