<p><ul><li>Ion-Assisted Deposition (IAD)</li><li>Electron Beam Evaporation</li><li>Sputtering</li><li>Vacuum Deposition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lens coating market includes various deposition techniques, each with unique properties. Ion-Assisted Deposition (IAD) enhances adhesion and durability through ion bombardment during coating. Electron Beam Evaporation involves vaporizing materials with an electron beam, creating thin films with precise control. Sputtering utilizes energetic particles to dislodge atoms from a target, leading to uniform coatings. Vacuum Deposition, a broader category, involves depositing materials in a vacuum to minimize contamination, ensuring high-quality optical surfaces. Each method caters to specific performance requirements in optics.</p></p>
