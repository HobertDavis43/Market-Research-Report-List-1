<p><ul><li>Rotary Friction Welder</li><li>Linear Friction Welder</li><li>Friction Stir Welder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The friction welder market is categorized into three main types: Rotary Friction Welders, which rotate one part against another to generate heat through friction; Linear Friction Welders, where components move linearly relative to each other to create joints; and Friction Stir Welders, which utilize a rotating tool to stir and bond materials solid-state without melting them. Each type offers unique advantages for different applications, such as precision joining and the ability to weld difficult-to-bond materials, thereby expanding their industrial use.</p></p>
