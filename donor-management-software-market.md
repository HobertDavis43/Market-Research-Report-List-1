<p><p>Donor Management Software (DMS) is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based DMS offers flexibility and accessibility, allowing users to manage donor information from anywhere with internet connectivity, often featuring automatic updates and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premise DMS is installed locally on the organization’s hardware, providing more control over data security and customization, but typically requiring higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types serve to enhance donor engagement and streamline fundraising processes.</p></p>
