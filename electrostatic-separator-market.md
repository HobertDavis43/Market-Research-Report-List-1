<p><p>The electrostatic separator market is divided into two primary types: dry and wet separators. Dry electrostatic separators utilize ambient air to separate materials based on their differing electrical charges, making them suitable for applications in recycling and mineral processing. In contrast, wet electrostatic separators use a liquid medium to enhance separation efficiency, often employed in wastewater treatment and ore processing. These market types cater to distinct industrial needs, optimizing material recovery and environmental sustainability in various sectors.</p></p>
