<p><strong>The Stationary POS Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Presentation Scanner</li><li>Bioptic Scanner</li><li>Mini-Slot Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary POS scanner market includes three primary types: presentation scanners, bioptic scanners, and mini-slot scanners. Presentation scanners are designed for hands-free scanning, allowing items to be scanned automatically as they are placed in front of the device. Bioptic scanners combine features of presentation and handheld models, providing versatility for scanning barcodes at various angles. Mini-slot scanners are compact devices ideal for limited counter space, effectively scanning items placed in a narrow slot for efficient transactions.</p></p>
