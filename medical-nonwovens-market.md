<p><strong>The Medical Nonwovens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical nonwovens market comprises several types based on their production methods: </p><p>1. **Drylaid** uses mechanical processes to create fibers, often resulting in strong and durable materials. </p><p>2. **Airlaid** involves dispersing fiber into a web using air, producing softer, more absorbent fabrics. </p><p>3. **Wetlaid** integrates fibers in a water-based process, yielding smooth, absorbent sheets suitable for various applications. </p><p>4. **Spunlaid** combines melt-blown and spunbond technologies, creating lightweight, high-strength fabrics, ideal for protective garments and medical disposables.</p></p>
