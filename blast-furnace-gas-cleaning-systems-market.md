<p><p>Blast furnace gas cleaning systems are essential for reducing pollutants from gas produced during iron making. The market is primarily divided into two types: dry cleaning systems and wet cleaning systems. Dry cleaning systems use filtration and electrostatic precipitators to remove particulates without water, making them cost-effective and efficient for handling large volumes. In contrast, wet cleaning systems utilize scrubbing techniques with liquid solutions to capture contaminants, ensuring higher removal rates, especially for soluble pollutants, but typically requiring more maintenance and operational costs.</p></p>
