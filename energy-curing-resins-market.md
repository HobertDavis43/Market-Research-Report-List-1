<p><strong>The Energy Curing Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radical Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Energy curing resins are divided mainly into two types: radical and cationic. Radical type resins cure through a free radical polymerization process, typically initiated by ultraviolet (UV) light, making them suitable for a range of applications including coatings and adhesives. Cationic type resins utilize a different mechanism, relying on cationic polymerization, often activated by UV light or heat, resulting in enhanced chemical resistance and lower emissions. Both types offer advantages depending on specific application needs, such as speed and durability.</p></p>
