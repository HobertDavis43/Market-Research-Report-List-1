<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) market is primarily categorized into two types: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP and Pitch-based CFRTP. PAN-based CFRTP utilizes polyacrylonitrile fibers, known for their high tensile strength and durability, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. Conversely, Pitch-based CFRTP is derived from coal tar or petroleum pitch, offering excellent thermal and electrical conductivity. Both types enhance material performance, providing lightweight, strong, and versatile alternatives for various industries, emphasizing efficiency and sustainability in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
