<p><ul><li>Woven</li><li>Nonwoven</li><li>Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive textiles market comprises three primary types: woven, nonwoven, and composites. Woven textiles involve interlacing threads, providing strength and durability for seat covers and interior fabrics. Nonwoven textiles are created by bonding fibers without weaving, offering lightweight and cost-effective solutions for insulation and soundproofing. Composites combine different materials, enhancing performance characteristics like resistance to wear and temperature. Together, these segments cater to diverse automotive applications, ensuring comfort, safety, and aesthetics in modern vehicle interiors.</p></p>
