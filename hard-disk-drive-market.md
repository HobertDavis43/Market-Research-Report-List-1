<p><p>A Hard Disk Drive (HDD) is a data storage device that uses magnetic storage to store and retrieve digital information. HDDs consist of one or more rotating disks coated with magnetic material, and data is read and written using read/write heads that move across the spinning disks. The rapid expansion of data centers and reliance on cloud services are further propelling demand for HDDs, particularly in enterprise applications where high capacity and affordability are crucial. </p><p>Moreover, trends such as the adoption of hybrid storage solutions and advancements in HDD technology, such as the development of heat-assisted magnetic recording (HAMR), are enhancing HDD capabilities. Enhanced reliability and increased capacity are key factors driving this growth.</p></p>
