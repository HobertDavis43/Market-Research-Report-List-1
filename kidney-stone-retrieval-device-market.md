<p><p>The kidney stone retrieval device market encompasses various types designed for efficient management of kidney stones. Lithotripters utilize shock waves to fragment stones into smaller pieces for easier passage. Stone removal devices, including baskets and forceps, are used surgically to extract stones directly from the urinary tract. Ureteral stents are placed in the ureter to facilitate urine flow and reduce obstruction caused by stones while promoting healing. Together, these devices enhance patient outcomes and streamline urological procedures.</p></p>
