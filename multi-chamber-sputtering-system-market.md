<p><ul><li>Load-lock type</li><li>Batch Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-Chamber Sputtering System market consists of two primary types: Load-lock type and Batch type. The Load-lock type enables efficient material transfer between chambers without breaking the vacuum, facilitating continuous production and reducing contamination risks. Conversely, the Batch type processes multiple substrates simultaneously within a single chamber, maximizing throughput for high-volume manufacturing. Both systems cater to diverse applications in industries such as semiconductor fabrication, optics, and flat-panel display manufacturing, enhancing deposition quality and efficiency.</p></p>
