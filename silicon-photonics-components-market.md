<p><p>Silicon photonics components encompass three main market types: lasers, modulators, and photodetectors. Lasers in this sector provide light sources integral for data transmission. Modulators are crucial for encoding information onto these light signals, enhancing communication efficiency. Photodetectors serve as the receivers, converting optical signals back into electrical signals for processing. Together, these components enable high-speed data transfer and support the growing demand for advanced computing systems and telecommunications, driving innovation in data centers and networking applications.</p></p>
