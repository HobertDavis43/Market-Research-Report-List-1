<p><p>The general LED driver market is categorized into single-channel and dual-channel types based on the number of output channels. Single-channel LED drivers are designed to control one LED or a group of LEDs, making them ideal for applications requiring simple on/off control or dimming. Dual-channel LED drivers can manage two separate channels simultaneously, allowing for more complex lighting setups and enhanced control features, such as color mixing or independent brightness adjustments, suitable for advanced lighting designs.</p></p>
