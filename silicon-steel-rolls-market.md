<p><strong>The Silicon Steel Rolls Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Steel Rolls market primarily consists of two types: Oriented Electrical Steel (GOES) and Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel (NGOES). GOES is designed for high-efficiency applications, offering superior magnetic properties in a specific direction, making it ideal for transformers and electric motors. In contrast, NGOES possesses isotropic characteristics, providing consistent performance in multiple directions, suited for various electrical applications such as generators and inductors. Both types are essential in enhancing energy efficiency and reducing losses in electrical devices.</p></p>
