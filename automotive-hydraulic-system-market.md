<p><p>The automotive hydraulic system market comprises several key types: hydraulic brakes, hydraulic clutches, hydraulic suspensions, and hydraulic tappets. Hydraulic brakes enhance vehicle safety by providing strong stopping power through fluid pressure. Hydraulic clutches facilitate smooth gear transitions in manual transmissions. Hydraulic suspensions improve ride comfort and handling by adjusting stiffness and height dynamically. Lastly, hydraulic tappets ensure precise valve timing and reduce engine noise by allowing for automatic adjustment of valve clearance, contributing to overall engine efficiency.</p></p>
