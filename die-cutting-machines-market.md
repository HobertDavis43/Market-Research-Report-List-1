<p><p>Die cutting machines are categorized into several types based on their operational mechanisms. Rotary die cutting machines utilize a continuous rotary motion, making them ideal for high-volume production with intricate designs. Platen die cutting machines use a flat surface to press the die against the material, suitable for thicker materials and precise cuts. Other types include clicker presses and digital die cutting machines, which offer flexibility for smaller runs or custom designs. Each type serves specific manufacturing needs in various industries.</p></p>
