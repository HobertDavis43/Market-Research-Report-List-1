<p><p>Rectangular portlights for boats come in two primary types: opening and standard. Opening portlights feature a hinged or sliding mechanism that allows for ventilation and easy access to fresh air, enhancing comfort onboard. In contrast, standard portlights have a fixed design, providing a robust, leak-proof window solution that offers a sleek appearance without the need for mechanical operation. Both types serve essential functions, protecting against water intrusion while allowing natural light into the cabin, thereby enhancing the boating experience.</p></p>
