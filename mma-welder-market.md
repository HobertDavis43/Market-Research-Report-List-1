<p><p>The MMA (Manual Metal Arc) welder market can be categorized into two primary types based on power source: AC (Alternating Current) and DC (Direct Current). AC power welders are typically used for welding aluminum and are favored for their ability to provide cleaner arcs. In contrast, DC power welders offer stable arcs and greater penetration, making them ideal for welding ferrous metals. Each type serves specific applications, impacting user choice based on material requirements and project demands.</p></p>
