<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ultrasound Elastography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Elastography (SE)</li><li>Dynamic Elastography With Harmonic Stimuli (DEHS)</li><li>Dynamic Elastography With Transient Stimuli (DETS)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound elastography system market comprises various types focused on assessing tissue stiffness. Static Elastography (SE) evaluates elasticity at a single point, providing a quick assessment. Dynamic Elastography with Harmonic Stimuli (DEHS) uses continuous mechanical waves to analyze tissue response to vibrations, offering real-time insights. Dynamic Elastography with Transient Stimuli (DETS) employs brief pressure changes to gauge tissue stiffness during transient events. Additionally, other niche techniques extend elastography applications, enhancing diagnostic capabilities across various medical fields.</p></p>
