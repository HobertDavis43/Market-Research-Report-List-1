<p><p>The preemergent herbicide market is categorized into two main types: selective and non-selective herbicides. Selective herbicides target specific weed species while leaving desired crops unharmed, making them ideal for agricultural use. In contrast, non-selective herbicides affect all plant species, providing a broader application for areas such as rights-of-way, industrial sites, and non-crop environments. Both types play crucial roles in weed management strategies, depending on the desired outcomes and the specific conditions of the land being treated.</p></p>
