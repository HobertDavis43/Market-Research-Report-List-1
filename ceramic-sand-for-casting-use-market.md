<p><p>The ceramic sand market for casting applications encompasses various technologies, including pre-coated sand, cold box, self-hardening sand, and lost foam casting. Pre-coated sand involves coating sand grains with resin, enhancing mold strength and finish. Cold box technology utilizes chemical binders for quick setting, yielding precise molds. Self-hardening sand relies on additives that harden without external heat, simplifying processes. Lost foam casting uses expendable foam patterns for complex shapes, providing excellent surface quality and dimensional accuracy in final products.</p></p>
