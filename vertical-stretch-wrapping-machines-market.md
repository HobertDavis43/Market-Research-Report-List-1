<p><p>Vertical stretch wrapping machines are classified into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention for loading the product and starting the wrapping process, making them suitable for smaller operations or varied product sizes. In contrast, automatic machines handle the entire wrapping process autonomously, enhancing efficiency and consistency for larger production lines. Both types are designed to improve packaging speed and protect products during storage and transportation, with the choice largely depending on production capacity and workflow requirements.</p></p>
