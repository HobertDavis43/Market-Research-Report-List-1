<p><p>The tire steel cord market consists of three main types: Normal Tensile (NT), High Tensile (HT), and Super Tensile (ST). NT steel cords are designed for standard performance, offering basic strength and flexibility. HT cords provide enhanced strength, suitable for high-performance tires, improving durability and load-bearing capacity. ST cords are engineered for superior tensile strength and low elongation, ideal for high-load and high-speed applications, ensuring optimal performance in premium tires. Each type caters to specific tire performance requirements.</p></p>
