<p><p>Polyester resins for powder coating are categorized based on their curing agents. Epoxy resin cure offers excellent adhesion and chemical resistance. TGIC (Triglycidyl Isocyanurate) cure enhances weather resistance and durability, making it popular for outdoor applications. HAA (Hybrids Amino Acid) cure provides good flexibility and toughness, suitable for various surfaces. Isocyanate cure improves cross-linking and resistance properties. Other market options include unique formulations tailored for specific applications, expanding versatility in industries like automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
