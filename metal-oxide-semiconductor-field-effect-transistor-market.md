<p><p>The Metal Oxide Semiconductor Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET) is a key component used in various electronic devices, acting as a switch or amplifier in circuits. Its structure consists of a gate, source, and drain, with a thin layer of insulating oxide separating the gate from the channel. This configuration allows for efficient control of electrical signals, making MOSFETs essential in power electronics, computing, and telecommunications.</p><p>The MOSFET market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient devices, advancements in semiconductor technology, and the rising adoption of electric vehicles and renewable energy systems. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period, reflecting the vital role MOSFETs play in modern electronics. </p><p>Recent trends include the development of Silicon Carbide (SiC) and Gallium Nitride (GaN) MOSFETs, which offer superior performance in high-voltage and high-temperature applications.
