<p><strong>The PTCA Balloon Dilatation Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi Compliant</li><li>Non Compliant</li><li>Compliant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTCA balloon dilatation catheters are categorized into three main types based on their compliance characteristics. Semi-compliant balloons expand under pressure but also retain some flexibility, making them suitable for a balance of stability and adaptability during dilatation. Non-compliant balloons maintain their shape and volume under higher pressures, providing targeted, controlled dilation without significant expansion. Compliant balloons, on the other hand, expand significantly as pressure increases, allowing for gentle dilation of compliant lesions, accommodating varying arterial sizes and shapes.</p></p>
