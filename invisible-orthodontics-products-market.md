<p><p>The invisible orthodontics products market primarily comprises three types: ceramic braces, clear aligners, and lingual braces. Ceramic braces are tooth-colored systems that blend with natural teeth, offering a discreet option while providing effective treatment. Clear aligners are removable, lightweight trays that gradually straighten teeth without the visibility of traditional braces. Lingual braces, placed on the inner surface of teeth, provide an unobtrusive solution for patients seeking orthodontic correction without external brackets. Together, these products cater to the growing demand for aesthetic orthodontic options.</p></p>
