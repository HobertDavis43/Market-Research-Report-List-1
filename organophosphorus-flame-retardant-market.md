<p><p>The organophosphorus flame retardant market is categorized into two main types: normal temperature type and high temperature type. Normal temperature flame retardants are designed for materials that are exposed to lower thermal conditions, providing effective protection against ignition at ambient temperatures. In contrast, high temperature flame retardants are formulated for use in environments experiencing elevated heat, offering enhanced stability and performance under extreme conditions. Both types play vital roles in improving fire safety across various applications, including textiles, plastics, and construction materials.</p></p>
