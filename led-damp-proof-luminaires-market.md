<p><p>LED damp proof luminaires are categorized based on their ingress protection ratings, primarily IP 65 and IP 66. IP 65 luminaires provide protection against dust and low-pressure water jets, making them suitable for wet areas like kitchens and bathrooms. IP 66 luminaires offer enhanced protection against powerful water jets and are ideal for harsher environments such as industrial settings or outdoor areas. Other types may include luminaires with lower IP ratings or specialized features for specific applications, catering to diverse installation needs.</p></p>
