<p><p>The plastic casters market includes various types based on material composition. PU (Polyurethane) casters offer durability and shock absorption, suitable for diverse surfaces. PA (Polyamide) casters are known for their strength and resistance to wear. PP (Polypropylene) casters are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, often used in lighter applications. PE (Polyethylene) casters provide good chemical resistance, making them ideal for industrial use. POM (Polyoxymethylene) casters offer excellent load-bearing capabilities and low friction, while PC (Polycarbonate) casters combine strength with impact resistance, suitable for demanding environments.</p></p>
