<p><p>The Digital Dental X-ray Sensors market is primarily categorized into intraoral and extraoral X-rays. Intraoral X-rays are designed for capturing images directly inside the mouth, providing detailed views of individual teeth and surrounding structures, crucial for diagnosing dental issues. Conversely, extraoral X-rays capture images from outside the mouth, including panoramic and cephalometric imaging, which are essential for assessing overall dental health and planning treatments. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy and patient care through advanced imaging technology.</p></p>
