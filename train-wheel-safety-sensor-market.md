<p><p>The train wheel safety sensor market is categorized into two main types: single wheel sensors and double wheel sensors. Single wheel sensors monitor individual wheels for abnormalities like excessive wear or overheating, enhancing precision in diagnostics. In contrast, double wheel sensors analyze pairs of wheels, providing a comprehensive assessment of wheel performance and alignment. Both types are crucial for ensuring train safety, minimizing downtime, and preventing accidents, thus improving overall operational efficiency in the railway industry.</p></p>
