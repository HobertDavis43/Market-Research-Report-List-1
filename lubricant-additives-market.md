<p><p>The lubricant additives market is primarily categorized into two types: single component additives and additive packages. Single component additives are individual substances such as anti-wear agents or detergents that enhance specific properties of lubricants. Additive packages, on the other hand, are pre-blended combinations of multiple additives designed to provide a balanced formulation for optimal lubrication performance. These packages simplify the blending process for manufacturers and ensure consistent quality in the final lubricant products, catering to diverse industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
