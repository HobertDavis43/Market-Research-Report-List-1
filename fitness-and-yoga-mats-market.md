<p><strong>The Fitness and Yoga Mats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TPE</li><li>NBR</li><li>EVA</li><li>Natural Rubber</li><li>PVC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fitness and yoga mats market includes various material types, each offering distinct features. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) mats are eco-friendly and lightweight, providing good cushioning. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) mats offer durability and are resistant to wear, ideal for high-intensity workouts. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) mats are soft, lightweight, and non-toxic, suitable for yoga. Natural rubber mats are biodegradable and provide excellent grip and cushioning. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) mats are affordable and versatile but less eco-friendly. Other materials include jute and cotton blends, appealing to niche markets.</p></p>
