<p><p>The infrared light angle sensor market is segmented into four main types based on wavelength: Short Wave IR (SWIR), Mid Wave IR (MWIR), Long Wave IR (LWIR), and Far Wave IR (FWIR). SWIR sensors operate in the 1-3 micron range, ideal for moisture detection. MWIR, spanning 3-5 microns, is suited for thermal imaging. LWIR, covering 8-12 microns, excels in temperature measurement. FWIR, beyond 12 microns, is utilized in specialized applications such as spectroscopy, enabling diverse industrial and research applications.</p></p>
