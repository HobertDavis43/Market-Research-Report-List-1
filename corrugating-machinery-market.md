<p><p>The corrugating machinery market is divided into two main types: wet end equipment and dry end equipment. Wet end equipment is used for the initial stages of corrugated board production, including the preparation of the paper sheets, applying adhesives, and forming the fluted layer. Dry end equipment handles the finishing processes, such as drying, cutting, and stacking the completed corrugated boards. Both segments are essential for enhancing production efficiency and ensuring quality in the manufacturing of corrugated packaging products.</p></p>
