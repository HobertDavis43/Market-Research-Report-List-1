<p><strong>The Dry Bulk Shipping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Time Charter</li><li>Voyage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry bulk shipping market consists of two primary types: the time charter and the voyage market. In a time charter, a shipowner leases their vessel to a charterer for a specified period, during which the charterer manages operations and pays for fuel and port expenses. In contrast, the voyage market involves the charterer hiring a vessel for a single trip between specified ports, with the shipowner covering operational costs. Both approaches provide flexibility and cater to varying shipping needs.</p></p>
