<p><p>Gelatin-Based Plasma Expanders are synthetic colloidal solutions used to restore blood volume in patients suffering from hypovolemic shock or undergoing surgery. These solutions contain gelatin, a product derived from collagen, which provides a temporary increase in intravascular volume, enhancing hemodynamic stability. Due to their biocompatibility and lower risk of allergic reactions compared to other volume expanders, gelatin-based products have gained popularity in clinical settings.</p><p>The Gelatin-Based Plasma Expander Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing number of surgical procedures, a rising prevalence of trauma cases, and advancements in healthcare technologies.
