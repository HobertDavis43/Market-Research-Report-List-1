<p><strong>The Voltage Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pass Filter</li><li>High Pass Filter</li><li>Band-pass filter</li><li>Band Rejection Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage filter market comprises four main types: Low Pass Filters (LPF), which allow signals below a certain frequency to pass while attenuating higher frequencies; High Pass Filters (HPF), which permit signals above a specified frequency and block lower frequencies; Band-pass Filters (BPF), which let through a specific range of frequencies while rejecting others; and Band Rejection Filters (BRF), which attenuate a specific frequency band while allowing others to pass. These filters are essential in various electronic applications for signal processing and noise reduction.</p></p>
