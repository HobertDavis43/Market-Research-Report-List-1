<p><strong>The Nickel Electroplating Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Agent</li><li>Electroplating Additive</li><li>Post-treatment Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nickel electroplating additive market comprises three main types: pretreatment agents, electroplating additives, and post-treatment agents. Pretreatment agents prepare the surface for optimal plating by enhancing adhesion and removing contaminants. Electroplating additives, including brighteners and leveling agents, improve the plating process by enhancing surface quality and uniformity during application. Post-treatment agents are used after plating to enhance the finish, improve corrosion resistance, and ensure long-lasting durability. Together, these additives optimize the electroplating process and enhance the final product quality.</p></p>
