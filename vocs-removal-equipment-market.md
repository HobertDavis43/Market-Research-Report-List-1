<p><p>The VOCs removal equipment market primarily features two types: burn technologies and oxidation methods. Burn technologies, such as thermal oxidizers, incinerate volatile organic compounds at high temperatures, converting them into carbon dioxide and water. In contrast, oxidation methods, including catalytic oxidizers, facilitate the chemical reaction of VOCs with oxygen, breaking them down into less harmful substances. Both approaches aim to mitigate environmental impact by reducing harmful emissions from industrial processes and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
