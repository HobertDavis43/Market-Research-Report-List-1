<p><p>The axial bone densitometry market primarily includes two technologies: Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) and Quantitative Computed Tomography (QCT). DEXA is widely used for measuring bone mineral density using low-dose X-rays, providing accurate assessments for osteoporosis diagnosis. QCT, on the other hand, employs advanced CT scans to evaluate bone density in a three-dimensional context, delivering detailed analyses of both cortical and trabecular bone. Together, these technologies enhance clinical decision-making in bone health management.</p></p>
