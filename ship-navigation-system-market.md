<p><strong>The Ship Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ENC</li><li>RNC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ship navigation system market is primarily divided into two types: Electronic Navigational Charts (ENC) and Raster Navigational Charts (RNC). ENCs are digitally formatted nautical charts that provide geospatial information for safe navigation, utilizing vector data for improved accuracy and updates. RNCs, on the other hand, are scanned images of traditional paper charts, offering less precision but retaining familiarity for some users. Both play crucial roles in enhancing maritime safety and operational efficiency, catering to various navigational needs.</p></p>
