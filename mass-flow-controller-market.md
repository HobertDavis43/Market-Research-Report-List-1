<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mass Flow Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mass flow controller (MFC) market is primarily divided into two types: direct type and indirect type. Direct type MFCs measure mass flow by using thermal or pressure differential methods, offering high accuracy and quick response times. Indirect type MFCs, on the other hand, utilize volumetric flow measurements and convert them to mass flow readings, typically being more cost-effective. Both types serve various industries, including semiconductor manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and aerospace, catering to diverse flow measurement needs.</p></p>
