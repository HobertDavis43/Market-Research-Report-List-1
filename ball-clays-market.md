<p><p>The ball clay market is divided into two main types based on kaolinite content. The 20-50% kaolinite ball clays contain a lower percentage of kaolinite, offering higher plasticity and workability, making them suitable for diverse ceramic applications, including tiles and tableware. In contrast, the 50-80% kaolinite ball clays have a higher kaolinite content, providing improved strength and reduced plasticity, ideal for high-performance ceramics, sanitary ware, and demanding industrial applications. Each type caters to specific needs in the ceramics sector.</p></p>
