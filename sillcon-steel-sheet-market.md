<p><p>The silicon steel sheet market is divided into two primary types: oriented and non-oriented. Oriented silicon steel sheets are specifically designed for high magnetic permeability and are used mainly in applications like transformers and electric motors, where efficiency is crucial. Non-oriented silicon steel sheets, on the other hand, exhibit uniform magnetic properties in all directions, making them suitable for a broader range of applications, including electric machines and generators. Each type serves distinct industries based on their magnetic characteristics.</p></p>
