<p><strong>The Vacuum Gauges for Semiconductor Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitance Diaphragm Gauge</li><li>Ionization Vacuum Gauge</li><li>Pirani Vacuum Gauge</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum gauges are essential for semiconductor equipment, measuring pressure in vacuum systems. Capacitance diaphragm gauges utilize a flexible diaphragm that responds to pressure changes, providing precise readings. Ionization vacuum gauges measure vacuum levels by ionizing gas molecules and detecting the resultant current. Pirani vacuum gauges rely on a heated wire that cools as pressure decreases, indicating vacuum strength. Other types may include thermal conductivity and quartz crystal gauges, each tailored to specific pressure ranges and applications in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
