<p><p>Heart Rate Monitors (HRMs) are devices that track and measure heart rate during physical activities, helping users optimize their workouts and monitor health. The market is primarily divided into two types: chest-strap HRMs and wrist-based HRMs. Chest-strap HRMs provide accurate readings by detecting electrical signals from the heart, making them ideal for serious athletes. In contrast, wrist-based HRMs offer convenience and versatility, tracking heart rate through optical sensors, appealing to casual fitness enthusiasts and those seeking a more integrated wearable experience.</p></p>
