<p><p>Textile auxiliaries for the polyester market are specialized chemicals that enhance the processing and performance of polyester fabrics. Thickeners increase viscosity in dye solutions, while binding agents help adhere dyes to the fabric. Cross-linking agents improve fabric durability and stability. Emulsifiers aid in mixing water and oil-based substances for uniform application. Fixing agents ensure color retention during washing, while dispersing agents prevent pigment agglomeration. Defoamers eliminate foam during processes, and other auxiliaries cater to specific textile needs.</p></p>
