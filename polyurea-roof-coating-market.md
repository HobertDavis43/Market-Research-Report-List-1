<p><p>The polyurea roof coating market is primarily divided into two types: pure polyurea and hybrid polyurea. Pure polyurea coatings are high-performance, offering excellent waterproofing and chemical resistance, making them suitable for demanding environments. In contrast, hybrid polyurea coatings combine polyurea with additional materials, enhancing flexibility and curing times while maintaining adequate protection.
