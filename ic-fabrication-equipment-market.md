<p><p>The IC fabrication equipment market encompasses various essential tools used in semiconductor manufacturing. Microlithography and mask making equipment create intricate patterns on silicon wafers. CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) equipment ensures a smooth wafer surface. Ion implanters introduce dopants to modify electrical properties. Deposition tools apply thin films, while etching and cleaning tools shape and purify materials. Process diagnostic equipment monitors and optimizes fabrication processes, while "other equipment" includes miscellaneous tools that support specific fabrication needs, collectively driving innovation in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
