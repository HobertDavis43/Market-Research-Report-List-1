<p><p>The radiotherapy instrument market encompasses various advanced technologies for cancer treatment. Gamma Knife utilizes focused gamma rays for precise brain tumor targeting. Linear Accelerators (LINAC) deliver high-energy x-rays or electrons to treat tumors, often used in outpatient settings. CyberKnife combines robotics and imaging to target tumors anywhere in the body. TomoTherapy integrates CT imaging with intensity-modulated radiotherapy for tailored treatment. Proton Knife and Heavy Ion Knife utilize charged particles for high precision in cancer treatment, minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue.</p></p>
