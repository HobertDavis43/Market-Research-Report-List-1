<p><p>The aerospace industry torque sensor market comprises primarily two types: rotary torque sensors and non-contacting torque sensors. Rotary torque sensors measure the torque applied to rotating components, providing precise feedback for performance and safety in aircraft systems. Non-contacting torque sensors utilize magnetic or optical technologies to measure torque without physical connection, reducing wear and enhancing reliability. Both types are essential for optimizing engine performance, ensuring safety, and facilitating advanced testing and monitoring in aerospace applications.</p></p>
