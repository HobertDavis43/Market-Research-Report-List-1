IPMs integrate power semiconductors, gate drivers, and protection features, enhancing efficiency and reducing design complexity in applications like motor drives and renewable energy systems. PIMs combine power devices and passive components in a compact format, optimizing performance and thermal management. Both types cater to the growing demand for energy efficiency, compact designs, and reliability in consumer electronics, contributing to innovative and sustainable solutions.</p></p>
