<p><p>One Time Password (OTP) tokens are security mechanisms that generate a unique, temporary passcode used to enhance authentication processes. The growth is further fueled by the expansion of digital platforms, e-commerce, and online banking, which necessitate robust security measures.</p><p>Recent trends include the integration of OTP technology with mobile applications and cloud services, as well as the use of biometric authentication methods alongside OTPs for enhanced security.
