<p><strong>The PV Pastes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Side Ag Paste</li><li>Rear Side Ag Paste</li><li>Rear Side Al Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV pastes market comprises three main types: front side Ag paste, rear side Ag paste, and rear side Al paste. Front side Ag paste is used to create conductive patterns on the solar cell's front, enhancing light absorption and efficiency. Rear side Ag paste is applied to the back, optimizing electrical conductivity. Rear side Al paste is utilized for applying aluminum, which reflects unused light back into the cell, boosting overall energy conversion. Each paste type contributes to improving solar cell performance.</p></p>
