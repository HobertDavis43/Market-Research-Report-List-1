<p><p>The business phone systems market is primarily divided into two types: on-premise and cloud-based systems. On-premise phone systems are installed locally within a company's infrastructure, providing full control over hardware and data but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based phone systems operate over the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, with maintenance handled by the service provider. Businesses often choose between these types based on their operational needs, budget, and technological preferences.</p></p>
